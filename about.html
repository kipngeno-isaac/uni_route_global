Our Heritage

A Vision Born from Experience

UniRoute Global Group was founded by education experts who recognized a significant gap in personalized guidance for African students. We saw brilliant minds struggling with complex application processes and opaque visa requirements.

Since our inception in 2018, we have evolved into a premier study abroad agency. We aren't just consultants; we are navigators, dedicated to bridging the gap between local Kenyan talent and global academic excellence.
