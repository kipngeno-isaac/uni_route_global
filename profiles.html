About the University

The University of Greenwich is located in the heart of London and Kent, boasting historic campuses that offer state-of-the-art facilities. For Kenyan students, Greenwich provides a welcoming international community with dedicated support services to help you transition into UK academic life smoothly.

Known for its excellence in business, engineering, and the creative arts, the university maintains strong links with industry leaders, providing students with invaluable networking and placement opportunities.
